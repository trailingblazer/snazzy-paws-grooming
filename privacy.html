Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At Snazzy Paws Grooming, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard personal information obtained through our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number) via contact or booking forms.
  • Pet information (name, breed, age) for appointment purposes.
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type) automatically via standard web processes.

How We Use Your Information

Data is used solely to:

  • Process bookings and respond to inquiries.
  • Improve our services and website experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties without consent, except where required by law.

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical measures including HTTPS encryption to protect your data. Forms are static HTML (no server-side storage) for this demonstration site.

Your Rights

In accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of changes.

For questions, contact us directly.
